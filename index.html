About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Peking University, Beijing, China. I am supervised by Prof. Shiliang Zhang. Before that, I received a master’s degree from the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2020 and received a bachelor’s degree in School of Electronic Information and Communications at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2017. My research interests include computer vision, and deep learning, focusing on multimodal learning, unsupervised learning, object tracking, domain adaptation, and domain generalization.
